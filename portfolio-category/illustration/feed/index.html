Kleurstaal

Kleurstaal is a Dutch translation for “Colour Palette/language”. For me personally, it represents a lifestyle where you & I have more missions in life than one. Where we’re willing to tap all in for all of them! Each colour represents a mission and eventually turns into a vibrant colour palette! Today I want to share these colour palettes with everyone free to experience these and invite you to use any colour palette, get inspired by it & eventually find your own unique Kleurstaal.
